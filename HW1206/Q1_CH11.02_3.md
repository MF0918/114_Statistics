**前置計算 (Preliminary Calculations)**

* **處理 1 ($i=1$):** $n_1 = 5$, $T_1 = 14$, $\sum x^2 = 42$
* **處理 2 ($i=2$):** $n_2 = 5$, $T_2 = 19$, $\sum x^2 = 79$
* **處理 3 ($i=3$):** $n_3 = 4$, $T_3 = 5$, $\sum x^2 = 9$
* **總體 ($Total$):**
    * 總樣本數 $N = 5 + 5 + 4 = 14$
    * 總和 $G = 14 + 19 + 5 = 38$
    * 總平方和 $\sum x_{ij}^2 = 42 + 79 + 9 = 130$

**平方和計算 (Sum of Squares Calculations)**

* **校正因子 (Correction Factor, CF):**
    $$CF = \frac{G^2}{N} = \frac{38^2}{14} = \frac{1444}{14} \approx \mathbf{103.1429}$$

* **總平方和 (Total SS):**
    $$SS_{Total} = \sum x_{ij}^2 - CF = 130 - 103.1429 = \mathbf{26.8571}$$

* **處理平方和 (Treatment SS, SST):**
    $$SST = \sum \frac{T_i^2}{n_i} - CF = \left( \frac{14^2}{5} + \frac{19^2}{5} + \frac{5^2}{4} \right) - 103.1429$$
    $$SST = (39.2 + 72.2 + 6.25) - 103.1429 = 117.65 - 103.1429 = \mathbf{14.5071}$$

* **誤差平方和 (Error SS, SSE):**
    $$SSE = SS_{Total} - SST = 26.8571 - 14.5071 = \mathbf{12.35}$$

**變異數分析表 (ANOVA Table)**

* **均方 (MS):**
    * 處理自由度 $df_{Tr} = k - 1 = 3 - 1 = 2$
    * 誤差自由度 $df_{E} = N - k = 14 - 3 = 11$
    * $MST = \frac{SST}{df_{Tr}} = \frac{14.5071}{2} = \mathbf{7.2536}$
    * $MSE = \frac{SSE}{df_{E}} = \frac{12.35}{11} \approx \mathbf{1.1227}$

* **F 統計量:**
    * $F = \frac{MST}{MSE} = \frac{7.2536}{1.1227} \approx \mathbf{6.46}$

| :--- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **處理 (Treatments)** | 2 | 14.5071 | 7.2536 | **6.46** |

#### **假設檢定 (Hypothesis Test) - SOP 6 步驟**

**Step 1: 設立假設 (Set up Hypotheses)**
* $H_0: \mu_1 = \mu_2 = \mu_3$
* $H_1:$ 至少有兩個平均數不相等 (At least two means differ)

**Step 2: 決定顯著水準 (Select Level of Significance)**
* $\alpha = 0.05$

**Step 3: 檢定統計量與分佈 (Determine Test Statistic and Distribution)**

$$F_{STAT} = \frac{MST}{MSE} \sim F_{k-1, N-k}$$
$$F_{STAT} \sim F_{2, 11}$$

**Step 4: 設立決策法則 (Establish Decision Rule)**
* 查表： $F_{0.05}(2, 11)$
* 臨界值 (Critical Value) = **3.98**
* **拒絕域 (Rejection Region):** 若 $F_{STAT} > 3.98$，則拒絕 $H_0$。


**Step 5: 計算檢定統計量 (Compute Test Statistic)**

$$F_{STAT} = \mathbf{6.46}$$

**Step 6: 統計決策與結論 (Decision and Conclusion)**
* **決策:** 因為 $6.46 > 3.98$，落在拒絕域內，故 **拒絕 $H_0$**。
* **結論:** 有充分證據顯示三種處理的平均數之間存在顯著差異。
* **P 值範圍:** 查表可知 $F_{0.01}(2, 11) = 7.21$ 且 $F_{0.05}(2, 11) = 3.98$。
    * 因為 $3.98 < 6.46 < 7.21$，故 **$0.01 < \text{p-value} < 0.05$**。
