#### **Ans.a)**

* **自變數 ($x$):** 收入 (Income)
* **應變數 ($y$):** 身體質量指數 (BMI)

---

#### **Ans.b)**

**1. 數據摘要與前置計算**

* 樣本數 $n = 6$
* $\sum x = 240.5$
* $\sum y = 162$
* $\sum x^2 = 12370.25$
* $\sum y^2 = 4446.22$
* $\sum xy = 6090.65$
* $\bar{x} = 240.5 / 6 \approx 40.0833$
* $\bar{y} = 162 / 6 = 27$

**2. 計算離差平方和 (Sum of Squares)**

$$S_{xx} = \sum x^2 - \frac{(\sum x)^2}{n} = 12370.25 - \frac{(240.5)^2}{6} = 12370.25 - 9640.0417 = \mathbf{2730.2083}$$

$$S_{xy} = \sum xy - \frac{(\sum x)(\sum y)}{n} = 6090.65 - \frac{(240.5)(162)}{6} = 6090.65 - 6493.5 = \mathbf{-402.85}$$

**3. 計算迴歸參數**

* **斜率 ($b_1$):**
    $$b_1 = \frac{S_{xy}}{S_{xx}} = \frac{-402.85}{2730.2083} \approx \mathbf{-0.1476}$$

* **截距 ($b_0$):**
    $$b_0 = \bar{y} - b_1\bar{x} = 27 - (-0.14755)(40.0833) = 27 - (-5.9144) = \mathbf{32.9144}$$

**4. 最小平方法迴歸線方程式**

$$\hat{y} = 32.9144 - 0.1476x$$

#### **Ans.c)**

**1. 計算各項平方和 (SS)**

* **總平方和 (Total SS):**    
    $$Total SS = S_{yy} = \sum y^2 - \frac{(\sum y)^2}{n} = 4446.22 - \frac{162^2}{6} = 4446.22 - 4374 = \mathbf{72.22}$$

* **迴歸平方和 (SSR):**    
    $$SSR = \frac{(S_{xy})^2}{S_{xx}} = \frac{(-402.85)^2}{2730.2083} = \frac{162288.1225}{2730.2083} \approx \mathbf{59.4417}$$

* **誤差平方和 (SSE):**    
    $$SSE = Total SS - SSR = 72.22 - 59.4417 = \mathbf{12.7783}$$

**2. 計算自由度 (df) 與均方 (MS)**

* **自由度:**    
    * $df_{R} = 1$    
    * $df_{E} = n - 2 = 6 - 2 = 4$    
    * $df_{T} = n - 1 = 5$    

* **均方:**    
    * $MSR = \frac{SSR}{df_{R}} = \frac{59.4417}{1} = \mathbf{59.4417}$
    * $MSE = \frac{SSE}{df_{E}} = \frac{12.7783}{4} \approx \mathbf{3.1946}$

**3. 檢定統計量與分佈 (Test Statistic)**

$$F_{STAT} = \frac{MSR}{MSE} = \frac{59.4417}{3.1946} \approx \mathbf{18.61}$$

$$F_{STAT} = \frac{MSR}{MSE} \sim F_{1, 4}$$
