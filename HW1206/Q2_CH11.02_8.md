**數據摘要 (Data Summary)**：
* **Method A**: $n_1=5$, Sum $T_1 = 380$, Mean $\bar{x}_1 = 76$
* **Method B**: $n_2=3$, Sum $T_2 = 199$, Mean $\bar{x}_2 \approx 66.33$
* **Method C**: $n_3=3$, Sum $T_3 = 261$, Mean $\bar{x}_3 = 87$
* **Overall**: $n=11$, Grand Sum $G = 840$

#### **Ans.a)**

**1. 計算變異數分析所需數值 (Calculate Sum of Squares)**

* **CM (Correction for Mean)**:  
    $$CM = \frac{G^2}{n} = \frac{840^2}{11} \approx 64145.4545$$

* **Total SS (Total Sum of Squares)** :  
    $$Total\ SS = \sum x_{ij}^2 - CM = 65286 - 64145.4545 = \mathbf{1140.5455}$$

* **SST (Sum of Squares for Treatments)**:  
    $$SST = \sum \frac{T_i^2}{n_i} - CM = \left(\frac{380^2}{5} + \frac{199^2}{3} + \frac{261^2}{3}\right) - 64145.4545$$
    $$= (28880 + 13200.3333 + 22707) - 64145.4545$$
    $$= 64787.3333 - 64145.4545 = \mathbf{641.8788}$$

* **SSE (Sum of Squares for Error)**:  
    $$SSE = Total\ SS - SST = 1140.5455 - 641.8788 = \mathbf{498.6667}$$

**2. 填寫變異數分析表 (ANOVA Table)**

| Source | df | SS | MS | F |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Treatments** | $2$ | $641.8788$ | $MST = 320.9394$ | $F_{STAT} = \mathbf{5.15}$ |
| **Error** | $8$ | $498.6667$ | $MSE = 62.3333$ | |
| **Total** | $10$ | $1140.5455$ | | |

(計算註記： $F = 320.9394 / 62.3333 \approx 5.1488$)

---

#### **Ans.b)**

**1. 設立假設 (Set up Hypotheses)**
* $H_0: \mu_A = \mu_B = \mu_C$
* $H_1$: At least one mean is different

**2. 檢定統計量與分佈 (Test Statistic)**  
$$F_{STAT} = \frac{MST}{MSE} = 5.15 \sim F_{2, 8}$$

**3. 拒絕域 (Rejection Region)**  
(設定顯著水準 $\alpha = 0.05$)  
查 F 表 ( $df_1=2, df_2=8, \alpha=0.05$)，臨界值 $F_{0.05} = 4.46$。  
**Reject $H_0$ if $F_{STAT} > 4.46$**  

**4. 結論 (Conclusion)**
* **Decision**: 因為 $F_{STAT} = 5.15 > 4.46$，落入拒絕域 $\rightarrow$ **Reject $H_0$**。  
* **Conclusion**: 數據提供了足夠的證據顯示這三種方法的平均分數有顯著差異。
