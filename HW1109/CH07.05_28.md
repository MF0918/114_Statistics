$p=0.70$  
$n=50$  

計算 $P(\hat{p} > 0.80)$  

$\sigma_{\hat{p}} = \sqrt{\frac{p(1-p)}{n}} = \sqrt{\frac{0.70 \times (1 - 0.70)}{50}} \approx 0.0648$    

Z標準化轉換：  
$P(\hat{p} > 0.80) = P(\frac{\hat{p} - p}{\sigma_{\hat{p}}} > \frac{0.80 - 0.70}{0.0648}) = P(Z > 1.54) = 1-P(Z<1.54)$  

查表:  
$P(Z < 1.54): 0.9382$  

$P = 1 - 0.9382 =$ `0.0618`
