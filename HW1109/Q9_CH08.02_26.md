# Ans:

$n=121$  
$x=79$  

$\hat{p} = \frac{x}{n} = \frac{79}{121} \approx$ `0.6529`

公式:  
$ME =  z^* \times SE =  z^* \times \sqrt{\frac{\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})}{n}}$

帶入計算:  
取 95% 信賴水準的 $z^*$ = 1.96  

$ME = 1.96 \times \sqrt{\frac{0.6529 \times (1 - 0.6529)}{121}} = 1.96 \times 0.0433 \approx$ `0.0849`
