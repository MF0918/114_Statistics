#  Ans:

- $n = 100$  
- $\overline{x} = 1020$   
- $s = 90$  

$\hat{\mu} = \overline{x} =$ `1020` (平均每小時產量)

$SE = \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}} = \frac{90}{\sqrt{100}} = \frac{90}{10} = 9 $

**誤差範圍（Margin of Error）**  
使用 95% 信賴水準: z = 1.96   
$ME = z \times SE = 1.96 \times 9 =$ `17.64`
